# Market Research Methodology

When conducting market research, follow this structure:

1. **Market Size & Growth** — TAM, SAM, SOM with revenue estimates and CAGR
2. **Key Players** — Top 5-10 competitors with market share, strengths, weaknesses
3. **Customer Segments** — Demographics, psychographics, pain points, willingness to pay
4. **Trends** — Technology, regulatory, consumer behavior shifts
5. **Opportunities** — Underserved segments, gaps in competitor offerings
6. **Risks** — Market saturation, regulatory, economic factors

Always cite sources and indicate confidence level (High/Medium/Low) for each claim.
